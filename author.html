About the author

         

My name is Jamie Graves and I am a double major in ICT and Political Science. I made this page to explore how social media influences politics and voting behaviors. This relates directly to my majors because many political scientists study the effect of media in general, not just social media, on voting habits.

I found this color palette online and decided to use it because it exemplifies the colors of both main political parites in the US. The added white and gold add more variety without being too distracting. This pallette follows the color theory of Triadic colors.
